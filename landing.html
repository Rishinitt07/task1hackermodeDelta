Rumble

  • This game is played by 2 player, player 1 is mentioned in red and the player 2 is mentioned in blue
  • Each player consists of 5 players namely: titan , tank , ricochet , semiricochet and cannon
  • The logic of the game is so simple , whenevr the cannon is clicked it shoots a bullet we have to saveguard the titan from the bullet
  • The ricochet and the semirecochet are there to change the bullet direction, when the bullet hits the non-reflecting side of the semiricochet , the semiricochet will get destroyed
  • the tank will protect the titan from the bullet, when the bullet hits the tank the bullet will get destroyed
  • you can resume , pause and reset the game by the buttons present below the game board
  • you can see the game history
  • each player is given 300s(5mins) , after the 5mins if a player is playing the game , then the opponent wins
